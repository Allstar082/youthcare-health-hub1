📖 Lesson 1: HIV & STI Prevention

The ABC Approach

In Rwanda, we promote the ABC method for safety:

  • A - Abstinence: The most effective way to prevent infection.
  • B - Be Faithful: Sticking to one partner whose status you know.
  • C - Condom Use: Using protection consistently and correctly.

What is PrEP?

PrEP is a daily pill that prevents HIV. It is available at many health centers in Kigali and across Rwanda for those who need it most.

🩸 Lesson 2: Your Menstrual Cycle

Your cycle is usually 21 to 35 days long. It has 4 main stages:

  1. Menstruation: When your period happens.
  2. Follicular: Your body prepares to release an egg.
  3. Ovulation: The "Fertility Window." This is when pregnancy is most likely.
  4. Luteal: Your body prepares for the next period.

Tip: Use our tracker to see which stage you are in!

🧠 Lesson 3: Mental Health & Stress

Being a student is stressful. Mental health is just as important as physical health.

  • Signs of Stress: Sleeping too much or too little, feeling irritable, or losing interest in hobbies.
  • How to cope: Talk to a trusted friend, practice deep breathing, and ensure you get enough sleep.
