Mental Health Toolbox

🌬️ The 4-7-8 Technique

Feeling anxious? Inhale 4s, Hold 7s, Exhale 8s. Repeat 4 times to calm your brain.

📝 Journaling

Write down 3 things you are grateful for today. It shifts your brain's focus from problems to progress.

📵 Digital Detox

Social media can cause "comparison trap." Try turning off your phone 1 hour before bed.

Need to talk to someone?

You don't have to carry the weight alone. Reach out to professional support:

  • Ndi-Haph: Mental health support line.
  • University Counselor: Visit the student affairs office for free counseling.
